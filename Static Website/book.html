Book name: Discrete Mathematics

Book description: Discrete Mathematics, written by Richard Johnsonbaugh, offers students plenty of opportunities to practice, apply, and demonstrate conceptual comprehension thanks to its almost 4,500 exercises. There are many uses for exercise sets, particularly in computer science. The nearly 650 worked examples give students quick access to information as they work. Understanding is strengthened by placing a lot of attention on how the many topics interact with one another. The text thoroughly demonstrates a variety of problem-solving approaches before giving readers the chance to put them to use.
